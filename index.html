AutoAuto


Empowering kids to drive their own tech pathways

AutoAuto is a scalable STEM learning platform that allows kids to start programming from an early age. Our unique curriculum integrates Python programming, machine learning and hand-held autonomous cars we equipped with sensors and cameras introducing children to the ways that software and hardware can be integrated. Students gradually level up to more advanced methods and projects that develop the skills needed to make their cars drive themselves.
